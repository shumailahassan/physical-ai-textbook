## My Work on the Physical AI Book

The Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics textbook represents my dedication to making advanced robotics concepts accessible to a broader audience. This comprehensive resource covers five interconnected modules:

- **Module 1**: The Robotic Nervous System (ROS2) - Communication and coordination framework
- **Module 2**: The Digital Twin (Gazebo & Unity) - Simulation and virtual testing environments
- **Module 3**: The AI-Robot Brain (NVIDIA Isaac) - AI perception, control, and decision-making
- **Module 4**: Vision-Language-Action (VLA) Systems - Multimodal integration for advanced capabilities
- **Module 5**: Complete Humanoid Robot Integration - Bringing all modules together

This textbook follows a spec-driven development approach, ensuring each component is well-defined before implementation. It combines theoretical foundations with practical implementations to provide readers with both understanding and hands-on experience.
