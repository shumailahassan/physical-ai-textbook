Introduction

Welcome to the comprehensive guide on Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics. This textbook explores the intersection of artificial intelligence and robotics, focusing on how AI systems can be integrated into physical robotic platforms.

In this book, we'll examine five interconnected modules covering ROS2, Digital Twins, NVIDIA Isaac, Vision-Language-Action systems, and complete integration. Each module builds upon the previous one, providing a holistic understanding of modern robotics.
